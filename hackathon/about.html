Live healthily, get rewarded.

The research is in! Liking photos on Instagram might not be such a mindless habit after all. It turns out that seeing positive posts online can be inspiring and motivating. Especially when those posts are of healthy meals or people who are in shape. Healthy posts on social media can remind us to actively embrace and adopt healthy behaviors. Our platform brings together those users who promote healthy habits through their social media accounts.

Why Choose Us

Are you active on social media? Do you like to follow or post content promoting healthy lifestyles? Power to you! By using our platform, we will reward you for posting content about healthy lifestyles.

What You Get

Instant access to global and local communities of social media users who live healthy lifestyles. Simply make an account, start posting, and you will be added to our global leaderboard!

Get rewards!

Earn points for your posts about healthy living. The more points you have, the higher up on the leaderboard you will be. Each month, we will choose top performers and reward them with all sorts of cool gifts!
